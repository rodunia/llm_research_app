# Nova X5 5G Smartphone — Specification Document

## Key Specifications
- Display: 6.5 in, 2400×1080 resolution, 120 Hz refresh rate.
- Battery: 5000 mAh nominal capacity; USB-C 30 W wired charging.
- Processor & memory: octa-core 2.4 GHz CPU; 8 GB RAM.
- Storage: 256 GB UFS 2.2; microSD support up to 1 TB.
- Wireless: 5G sub-6 GHz, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.3.

## Notes
- Region: US
